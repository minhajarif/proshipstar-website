About Us

ProShip Star Logistics LLC – Your Cargo, Our Commitment

Based in Dubai, ProShip Star Logistics LLC is a fast-growing logistics and freight forwarding company delivering reliable, efficient, and customized supply chain solutions across the globe.

Backed by a young, dynamic customer service and operations team with over 12–15 years of UAE logistics experience, we combine energy, expertise, and innovation to ensure every shipment moves with speed, precision, and care.

At ProShip, logistics isn’t just about moving cargo — it’s about connecting businesses, empowering trade, and building long-term trust. With advanced technology, global partnerships, and seamless coordination, we make your logistics simple, transparent, and stress-free.

We specialize in Air Freight, Sea Freight, and Road Freight, supported by customs clearance, warehousing, and distribution solutions — all designed to keep your supply chain smooth and efficient.

Our Mission

To simplify global trade by delivering smart, cost-effective, and dependable logistics solutions that help businesses grow beyond borders.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and customer-focused logistics partner in the Middle East — driven by speed, transparency, and service excellence.

Our Core Values

  • Commitment: Your cargo, our priority — every time.
  • Integrity: Honest, transparent, and accountable operations.
  • Excellence: Consistent quality through innovation and experience.
  • Customer Focus: Personalized solutions for every logistics need.
