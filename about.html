ProShip Star Logistics LLC – Your Cargo, Our Commitment

Based in Dubai, ProShip Star Logistics LLC is a fast-growing logistics and freight forwarding company delivering reliable, efficient and customized supply chain solutions across the globe.

Backed by a young, dynamic customer service and operations team with over 12–15 years of UAE logistics experience, we combine energy, expertise and innovation to ensure every shipment moves with speed, precision and care.

At ProShip, logistics isn’t just about moving cargo — it’s about connecting businesses, empowering trade and building long-term trust. With advanced technology, global partnerships and seamless coordination, we make your logistics simple, transparent and efficient.

Whether you are shipping urgent air freight, full container loads, road freight within the region or require dependable warehousing and customs clearance, ProShip Star Logistics delivers tailored solutions for your business.

Your cargo, our commitment.
