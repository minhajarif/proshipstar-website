Custom Clearance

Fast, Accurate & Hassle-Free Customs Clearance Services

ProShip Star Logistics LLC delivers fast, smooth, and fully compliant Customs Clearance services across all UAE ports, airports, and borders. Our experienced clearance team ensures your shipments move through Customs quickly without delays, penalties, or errors.

From documentation and approvals to duty payments and inspections — we handle the entire import and export clearance process on your behalf.

Our Customs Clearance Services Include

  • Import & export clearance (Air, Sea & Road)
  • Complete documentation & paperwork handling
  • HS Code classification & duty/tax calculations
  • Dubai Trade, Mirsal II & Port Community System support
  • Inspection coordination with Customs & other authorities
  • FIRS, MOCCAE, Municipality & Health approvals
  • Free zone & mainland clearance

Why Choose ProShip For Customs Clearance?

  • Experienced clearance team with UAE expertise
  • Fast approvals with minimal delays
  • Accurate and compliant documentation
  • Dedicated support for complex shipments
  • Strong knowledge of UAE import/export laws

Whether it’s commercial shipments, personal cargo, or urgent imports — our Customs Clearance specialists ensure smooth processing and complete compliance every time.
