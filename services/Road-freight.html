Road Freight

Reliable & Professional Road Transport Across UAE & GCC

ProShip Star Logistics LLC provides dependable and flexible Road Freight solutions across the UAE and GCC region. Whether it’s palletized cargo, commercial goods, bulk shipments, or temperature-controlled items — we ensure safe, timely, and smooth transportation through our wide road network.

With a fleet of trusted transport partners and experienced drivers, we handle all domestic and cross-border deliveries with complete safety and efficiency.

Our Road Freight Services Include

  • UAE & GCC-wide transportation
  • Full Truck Load (FTL) & Less Truck Load (LTL)
  • Temperature-controlled & refrigerated transport
  • Express road delivery for urgent shipments
  • Pickup & last-mile distribution
  • Door-to-door service with documentation support

Why Choose Road Freight With Us?

  • Safe & secure transportation of all cargo types
  • Skilled drivers & reliable partner fleet
  • Fast turnaround & priority delivery options
  • Dedicated road operations team for support
  • Affordable and cost-effective transit solutions

Whether you're shipping within the UAE or across GCC (Saudi Arabia, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait), our Road Freight team ensures fast, smooth, and efficient cargo movement with complete visibility and care.
