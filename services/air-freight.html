Air Freight

Our Air Freight services are designed for time-sensitive and high-value cargo that cannot wait. With access to leading airlines, flexible routing options, and competitive transit times, we ensure your shipments move quickly and safely across the globe.

From airport-to-airport to full door-to-door solutions, ProShip Star Logistics offers tailored air freight services, including consolidation, priority cargo, and charter options. Our experienced team manages documentation, tracking, and coordination, so you stay informed at every stage.

Whether it’s urgent spare parts, electronics, fashion, or general cargo, our air solutions balance speed, cost, and reliability to keep your supply chain running on time.
