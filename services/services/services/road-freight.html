Road Freight

Our Road Freight services cover domestic distribution within the UAE as well as cross-border transportation across the GCC and neighbouring regions.

We handle full truckload (FTL) and less-than-truckload (LTL) cargo, offering reliable transit schedules, GPS-enabled vehicles, and trained drivers to ensure safe and on-time delivery.

From pallets to project cargo, ProShip Star Logistics provides flexible trucking solutions tailored to your cargo type, route, and time requirements, keeping your supply chain moving end-to-end.
