OUR SERVICES

ProShip Star Logistics > Services

Express

Time-critical courier and express solutions for urgent documents and parcels. Door-to-door pickup and delivery options for domestic and international movements.

Air Freight

Priority and economy air freight services with space commitments on leading airlines. Ideal for high-value and time-sensitive cargo that requires fast transit and reliable handling.

Sea Freight (FCL / LCL)

Full container load (FCL) and less-than-container load (LCL) options connecting major ports worldwide. Cost-effective solutions for bulk cargo, with flexible schedules and competitive rates.

Road Freight

Reliable inland trucking solutions for local UAE distribution and GCC over-land movements. Options for full truckload (FTL) and part loads, with GPS-tracked vehicles and professional drivers.

Warehousing

Secure warehousing facilities with flexible storage space, inventory management and value-added services such as pick-and-pack, labeling, palletization and consolidation.

Custom Clearance

End-to-end customs documentation and brokerage services for imports and exports. Our team ensures compliance with UAE customs regulations, reducing delays and avoiding penalties.
